# T8 v1.0.0

## Termination (Level 8)

### 📌 Summary
Extreme exit barriers, instant arbitrary termination, no data rights.

### 👤 What This Means For You
It's extremely difficult and expensive to leave this service, with massive penalties and long lock-in periods. Your account can be terminated instantly without warning or reason. You have essentially no rights to your data after termination and it may be deleted or retained at provider's whim.

### 📜 Legal Text
User termination requires 180 days notice and payment of liquidated damages equal to full contract value. Provider may terminate any account instantly without cause, notice, or appeal. All user content becomes provider property upon termination. No data export, access, or deletion rights granted. Users indemnify provider for any termination-related claims. Perpetual license to user content survives termination. Multi-year automatic renewals with compounding penalties.

### 🔍 Examples
- Exploitative contracts
- Services with history of data hostage
- Platforms with anti-competitive practices
